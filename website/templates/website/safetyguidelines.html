⚠️ Important Safety Notice

Our Trust Indicators are confidence-building features only. They do NOT verify user identity, conduct background checks, or guarantee safety. Always exercise caution and conduct your own due diligence when interacting with other members.

1. Understanding Trust Levels

What Trust Levels Mean:

  • Basic Trust: New member with minimal platform activity
  • Enhanced Trust: Active member with positive community interaction history
  • Premium Trust: Long-term member with consistent positive engagement

What Trust Levels Do NOT Mean:

  • Verified identity or background information
  • Criminal record clearance
  • Financial stability verification
  • Safety guarantee for in-person meetings

2. Online Conduct and Discretion

We uphold a strict standard of sophisticated decorum. All communications must be respectful and confidential. We strictly prohibit harassment, solicitation, and the sharing of private member information.

  • Protect Your Identity: Never share full personal identification (e.g., home address, passport details, specific work location) in early communication.
  • Be Mindful of Sharing: Assume conversations are private, but exercise caution about sensitive information.
  • Report Violations: Use the reporting function immediately if any member's behavior violates our code of conduct.

3. Meeting in Person

Your transition from virtual connection to meeting in person should be planned with care and prudence. These guidelines ensure your comfort and safety during first introductions:

  • Meet in Public: Always choose a public, well-populated venue (e.g., a high-end restaurant or private club lounge) for the first few meetings.
  • Inform a Trusted Contact: Share the time, date, and location of your meeting with a friend or associate. Arrange for a check-in call after a specified time.
  • Arrange Your Own Transportation: Maintain independence by managing your own transport to and from the venue.

4. Financial Security

Synergy is a platform for genuine connection, not financial solicitation. We enforce a zero-tolerance policy against attempts to solicit funds or gifts.

  • Never Send Money: Do not transfer money, provide access to bank accounts, or send gifts to anyone you meet online, regardless of the story they tell.
  • Be Wary of Urgent Requests: Scammers often create urgent financial crises. Report any such requests immediately.

5. Privacy and Data Protection

Your privacy is paramount. We employ industry-leading security measures to protect your data and ensure your information remains confidential.

  • Secure Platform: All data is encrypted and stored on secure servers with multiple layers of protection.
  • Controlled Sharing: You control what information is visible to other members and when.
  • Right to Delete: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

If you feel unsafe or have been violated, please contact our Trust & Safety team immediately at safety@synergy.com.
