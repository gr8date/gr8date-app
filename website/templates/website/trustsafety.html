Building Member Confidence

We employ various features to help members make informed decisions about their connections. These confidence indicators are designed to enhance your experience while maintaining appropriate expectations.

  • Activity Indicators: See member engagement levels and responsiveness.
  • Community Standing: Members with positive interaction histories may display higher confidence levels.
  • Photo Guidelines: All profile photos are reviewed for appropriateness and clarity.
  • Remember: These are confidence features only, not verification of identity or background.

Platform Security Features

We implement security measures to protect your platform experience and personal information from unauthorized access.

  • Encrypted Communications: Platform messages are protected using industry-standard encryption.
  • Privacy Controls: Features like Private Browsing help you manage your visibility.
  • Data Protection: We follow industry practices to safeguard your information.
  • No Guarantees: While we employ security measures, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

Community Standards & Conduct

We maintain standards for respectful interaction and provide tools to address concerns within our community.

  • Reporting System: Easy-to-use tools allow members to report concerns directly to our team.
  • Community Guidelines: Clear standards for respectful interaction and appropriate conduct.
  • Responsive Action: We review reports and take appropriate actions to maintain community standards.
  • Your Responsibility: Ultimately, you are responsible for your interactions and safety.
