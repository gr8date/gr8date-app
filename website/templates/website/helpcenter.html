Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +

To reset your password, click on the "Sign In" button and then select "Forgot Password". You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password securely.

Can I pause my membership? +

Yes, you can pause your membership for up to 3 months. Navigate to your account settings, select "Membership", and choose the "Pause Membership" option.

How do I report inappropriate behavior? +

You can report any inappropriate behavior directly from a user's profile by clicking the three-dot menu and selecting "Report". Our safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
