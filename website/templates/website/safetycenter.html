⚠️ Important Safety Notice

Our Trust Indicators are confidence-building features only. They do NOT verify user identity, conduct background checks, or guarantee safety. Always exercise caution and conduct your own due diligence when interacting with other members.

1. Understanding Our Safety Approach

We employ multiple layers of protection to create a secure environment while emphasizing that ultimate safety responsibility lies with each member.

2. Trust & Confidence Features

What Our Indicators Mean:

  • Profile Confidence: Based on member activity and positive community engagement
  • Platform Security: Encrypted communications and data protection measures
  • Community Monitoring: Active review of reported content and behavior

Important Limitations:

  • No identity verification or background checks
  • No criminal record clearance
  • No safety guarantees for in-person meetings
  • Members must conduct their own due diligence

3. Meeting in Person Safely

When transitioning from virtual connection to meeting in person, follow these essential safety protocols:

  • Meet in Public: Always choose well-populated, reputable venues for initial meetings
  • Inform Trusted Contacts: Share meeting details with friends or family
  • Arrange Your Own Transportation: Maintain independence with personal transport
  • Stay Sober: Keep clear judgment during initial encounters
  • Trust Your Instincts: Leave immediately if you feel uncomfortable

4. Online Conduct & Privacy

Maintain your privacy and practice sophisticated decorum in all interactions:

  • Protect Personal Information: Never share home address, financial details, or specific work location early in communication
  • Use Platform Messaging: Keep conversations on our encrypted platform initially
  • Control Your Visibility: Utilize privacy settings to manage your profile visibility
  • Report Concerns Immediately: Use reporting functions for any behavior violations

5. Financial Security

Synergy is for genuine connections, not financial transactions. Protect yourself from financial exploitation:

  • Never Send Money: Do not transfer funds or provide financial access to anyone met online
  • Beware of Urgent Requests: Scammers often create fake emergencies requiring money
  • No Gift Solicitations: Report any requests for gifts or financial assistance
  • Investment Offers: Be wary of investment opportunities or business proposals

6. Data Protection & Privacy

Your privacy is fundamental to our platform's integrity:

  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard encryption for all stored data
  • Controlled Information Sharing: You decide what information is visible and when
  • Right to Delete: Permanent account deletion available at any time
  • No Data Selling: We never sell or share your personal information with third parties

Essential Safety Questions

How do I report suspicious behavior? +

Use the reporting function on any profile or message. Click the three-dot menu and select "Report." Our safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action.

What should I do if I feel unsafe during a meeting? +

Immediately remove yourself from the situation. Contact local authorities if necessary, then report the incident to our safety team. We recommend having an exit strategy and check-in system for all meetings.

How are trust levels determined? +

Trust indicators reflect platform activity patterns and positive community engagement history. They are confidence features only and do not represent verified identity, background checks, or safety guarantees.

Can someone verify if a member is who they claim to be? +

No. We do not conduct identity verification or background checks. Our trust indicators are based on platform behavior patterns only. Always conduct your own due diligence and verify information independently.
